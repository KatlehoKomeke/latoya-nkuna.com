About me

Hi there, my name is Latoya Cyrilene Nkuna.

For the past four years I have been studying to become a qualified ICT Specialist and English teacher and I am proud to say I have finally reached the final year to obtaining that.

I believe that teaching is about developing strong relationships into learners passions and providing relevancy to learners which will allow them to make connections with the curriculum. I believe that I champion what is best for learners in all decisions and I model lifelong learning in my personal and professional life.

To learn more about what I have learnt and view the work I have collected throughout the past four years, take a look at my portfolio.
